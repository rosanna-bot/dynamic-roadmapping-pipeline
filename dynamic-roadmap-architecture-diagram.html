AI Intelligence Layer & Outputs

🤖 AI Sidekick — Onboarding

1 Opens full-screen on first visit. Suggests artifacts to connect based on roadmap description.
2 User confirms/rejects: Jira project, strategy docs, Miro boards, analytics tools.
3 AI ingests connected sources and starts populating Pulse, Roadmap, and Signals.
4 Sidekick remains available as conversational copilot for ongoing roadmap refinement.
