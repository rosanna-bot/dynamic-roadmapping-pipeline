The Problem

PLs and PMs are operating on incomplete and stale information

Strategy lives in slides. Feedback lives in Gong. Execution lives in Jira. The PM is the manual integration layer stitching it all together.

  • Roadmaps are static snapshots that decay the moment they're created
  • They don't know if their strategy is the right one — execution and strategy are siloed
  • It is laborious and slow to collate and synthesise insights
  • Decisions are driven by opinion, politics, or the loudest voice
